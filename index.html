About Jon

Hailing from Great Falls, MT, Jon began his career building luxury homes in 1991. Having been given the opportunity to work on many beautiful homes across Montana, he has gained unparalleled experience in all aspects of residential contracting in Big Sky Country.
With a reputation for excellent service and an eye for detail, he brings a set of skills particularly suited to delivering unique requests and custom designs. Serving the Flathead Valley - Kalispell, Whitefish, Bigfork and Columbia Falls.
